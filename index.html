Discover Victoria with Ausjet’s Chartered Tours

Victoria offers a host of spectacular coastal, cultural, and wildlife experiences within an easy flight of sophisticated Melbourne

With the foodie, cultural and sports-loving city of Melbourne as its hub, experience Victoria’s diverse adventures all within just a few hours flight. Enjoy intimate encounters with Australian wildlife, one of the world’s great coastal flights, beautiful gardens and relaxing vinyards, plus superb food, wine and coffee throughout the state. Victoria is a mecca for the adventurous but also the leisurely - and what better way to explore the state than with a private charter from Ausjet Aviation Group or one of their partnered companies.
